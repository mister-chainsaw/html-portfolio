The Best Video Games According to Mark

My top 3 video games of all time

Red Dead Redemption

Probably my favorite of all time. The open world, the nostalgia of it, I have a lot of memories playing it so that's why this is ranked first.

GTA IV

This one is a classic. Another open world great, like Red Dead Redemeption but a more modern version.

Fallout New Vegas

Sticking with the open world theme, this RPG is a classic. You could play this game theoretically forever, since every conversation you have in-game can lead to different outcomes.
